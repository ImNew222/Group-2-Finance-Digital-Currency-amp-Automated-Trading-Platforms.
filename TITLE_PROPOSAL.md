## 1. Statement of the Problem

As Neo-Cebu City transitions into a fully digital smart city by 2026, its financial infrastructure — including digital currency systems (CebuCoin), AI-powered automated trading platforms, digital wallets, and smart contracts — faces an unprecedented wave of emerging cyber threats. These include AI-driven attacks, deepfake fraud, quantum computing vulnerabilities, and advanced social engineering campaigns collectively known as "Hydra-Pulse." Current security measures, built on 2024–2025 standards, are insufficient to protect billions of pesos in daily digital transactions and the financial data of over 2 million citizens.

Specifically, this study aims to answer the following questions:

1. What are the current and emerging cybersecurity threats targeting digital currency systems and automated trading platforms in a smart city environment?
2. What vulnerabilities exist across the hardware, software, and human layers of Neo-Cebu's financial infrastructure that could be exploited by these threats?
3. How can Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC), Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA), and AI-powered threat detection be integrated into a unified cybersecurity system to effectively mitigate these threats?

**Scope and Boundaries:**
This study is limited to the finance sector of Neo-Cebu City, specifically digital currency and automated trading systems. It does not cover other smart city sectors such as healthcare, transportation, or education.

## 3. Core Features

The proposed system, **HerculesVault**, is a multi-layered cybersecurity framework consisting of three integrated pillars:

### 🔐 Pillar 1: Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)
- Upgrades all encryption from RSA/ECC to quantum-resistant algorithms (CRYSTALS-Kyber, CRYSTALS-Dilithium)
- Protects CebuCoin transactions, smart contract signatures, and stored financial records from quantum computing threats
- Eliminates the "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" attack strategy

### 🚫 Pillar 2: Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA)
- Implements "never trust, always verify" across all financial systems
- Enforces micro-segmentation, least-privilege access, and continuous multi-factor authentication
- Ensures that a breach in one system (e.g., trading) does not compromise others (e.g., wallets)

### 🤖 Pillar 3: AI-Powered Threat Detection (Sentinel AI)
- Real-time monitoring of all transactions for anomaly detection
- Deepfake detection for video/voice-based authorization attempts
- Behavioral biometrics to identify compromised accounts even with stolen credentials
- Predictive threat intelligence using global threat databases and dark web monitoring

## 5. Why "HerculesVault"?

The system is named after **Hercules** (Roman mythology), the hero who slew the **Lernaean Hydra** — a multi-headed serpent where cutting one head caused two more to grow. "Vault" represents the secure protection of Neo-Cebu's financial assets.

This mirrors our cybersecurity challenge:
- **Hydra-Pulse** = the multi-headed cyber threat (AI attacks, deepfakes, quantum threats, social engineering)
- **HerculesVault** = our multi-layered defense system that eliminates each threat permanently and vaults (protects) the city's digital finance

Just as Hercules used **strategy, strength, and teamwork** (with his nephew Iolaus) to defeat the Hydra, our system uses **PQC, Zero Trust, and Sentinel AI** working together to ensure no threat can regenerate.
